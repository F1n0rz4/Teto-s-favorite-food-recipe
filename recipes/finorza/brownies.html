Brownies

Description

Brownies have always been my go-to comfort treat. Every time I step into a bakery, my eyes immediately search for that perfect tray of rich, chocolatey goodness. There’s just something irresistible about the combination of a soft, tender crumb and a warm, gooey center that melts with every bite. What makes brownies even better is how simple they are to create at home. With just a handful of basic ingredients, you can whip up a batch that tastes every bit as indulgent as the ones from your favorite bakery. This recipe captures everything I love about brownies, it's sweet, fudgy, and incredibly satisfying. If you’re craving a dessert that’s easy, comforting, and guaranteed to impress, these brownies are the perfect choice.

Ingredients

The ingredients is very simple and you can make it in your home

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled

  • 1 cup granulated sugar

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • 2 large eggs

  • ½ cup all-purpose flour

  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder

  • ¼ tsp salt

  • 1 ½ cups chocolate chips (optional, for extra fudginess)

Instructions

here's the simple instructions that you can follow

  1. Preheat Oven & Prepare Pan: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Lightly grease a 9x9 inch metal baking pan and line it with parchment paper, leaving some overhang on the sides for easy removal later.

  2. Combine Wet Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the slightly cooled melted butter, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth. Whisk in the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

  3. Add Dry Ingredients: Stir in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter; stop as soon as no dry streaks of flour are visible.

  4. Fold in Chocolate Chips: Gently fold in the chocolate chips with a spatula if desired. The batter will be thick.

  5. Bake: Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until the edges are set and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs attached.

  6. Cool Completely: Place the pan on a wire rack and allow the brownies to cool completely before slicing. This is crucial for achieving a proper fudgy texture and clean cuts
